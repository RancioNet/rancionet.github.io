Where is it?

Prepositions of place:

In: Dentro / En.
On: Sobre / Arriba.
Under: Debajo / Abajo.
Behind: Detrás / Atrás.
Between: Entre medio.
Next to: Al lado.
Near: Cerca.

Examples:

Where is the ball?

It’s between the boxes.

Where is the cat?

It’s on the chair.
